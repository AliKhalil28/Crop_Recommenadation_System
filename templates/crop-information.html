Learn About Crops

Discover important details about various crops, including their growth cycle, soil requirements, and common pests and diseases. This information will help you make informed decisions based on the recommendations from our system.

Wheat

Growth Cycle: 100-120 days

Soil Requirements: Loamy or clayey soil with good drainage

Common Pests/Diseases: Aphids, Leaf Rust, Wheat Smut

Rice

Growth Cycle: 120-140 days

Soil Requirements: Clayey or loamy soil with standing water

Common Pests/Diseases: Brown Plant Hopper, Rice Blast

Maize

Growth Cycle: 90-120 days

Soil Requirements: Well-drained fertile soil

Common Pests/Diseases: Corn Earworm, Northern Corn Leaf Blight

Soybean

Growth Cycle: 80-100 days

Soil Requirements: Loamy soil with good organic matter

Common Pests/Diseases: Soybean Aphids, Soybean Cyst Nematode

Cotton

Growth Cycle: 150-180 days

Soil Requirements: Well-drained loamy or sandy soil

Common Pests/Diseases: Boll Weevil, Cotton Wilt

Sugarcane

Growth Cycle: 10-12 months

Soil Requirements: Fertile alluvial soil with good drainage

Common Pests/Diseases: Stem Borer, Red Rot
